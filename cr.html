Market wall size = followers · color = today's move · CR clients ringed · click to compare

Methodology. Streamer University 2026 was announced by Kai Cenat on July 6, 2026: 120 students admitted from 1M+ applications; campus runs July 15-20, 2026. Follower "prices" marked ● were read from public trackers (Streams Charts, TwitchTracker, Social Blade) on July 13, 2026; figures marked ≈ are estimates from the most recent public data and can be off. The 7d Δ column is computed from this hub's own recorded history once it exists (marked without ≈); until then it shows weekly estimates converted from 30-day tracker rates (marked ≈). RIZPY's numbers come straight from his 5-minute samples. Sparklines without recorded history are illustrative; the endpoints are the data. With live mode connected, Twitch numbers come straight from the official Twitch API in your browser, and % change is computed from daily snapshots this page saves locally. That part is real data, not estimates. Instagram/TikTok have no free API, so those stay snapshots. Subscriber counts: Twitch keeps subs private to each broadcaster (public "sub counts" on tracker sites are estimates), so they are not shown here; YouTube subscriber and view counts are public and get tracked once a YouTube API key is added to the collector. TikTok, Instagram and X have no free official follower API. Roster per the official reveal and fan-compiled lists; a handful of the 120 names were announced on stream only.
